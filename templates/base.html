About Us

We are a professional detailing and car washing team from Ukraine. We have been working in this field for more than 10 years and coped well with our tasks. But suddenly the war came and we ended up in Romania. Having left our homeland and found ourselves in a new world for us, we could not leave the cause to which we have been devoted for a long time. Therefore, it was decided to start all over again here. We have resumed our business and will be happy to provide decent service to the country that has received us so warmly.
